# NomadFi

NomadFi is a privacy-first cross-border financial decision app for people whose financial lives span more than one country.

It helps users:

- choose the right card or payment method
- reduce avoidable FX and fee leakage
- manage credit-card timing and balance freshness
- organize financial context without requiring full manual bookkeeping

## Platform

NomadFi is built for **iPhone and iPad**.

## Privacy

NomadFi is designed so that financial data stays on device unless you explicitly export or share something yourself.

- no analytics
- no advertising SDKs
- no third-party tracking
- optional Apple FinanceKit access only with explicit user consent on eligible devices
